Jackie Chan

One of the Greatest Martial Artists Ever

A legend to many a-kind,

a better man is hard to find,

though personally I know him not,

he's one hard to be forgot.

A striking figure and a kind smile,

his legacy will last a long while.

Understated in his worth,

he himself thinks the worst.

But love him many still do,

You have to know who,

The man who has made us laugh,

his talent stands unsurpassed.

Through the age we have adored,

many collect his work like hordes.

One to teach fun and defence,

I’ve not known one like him since.

The one and only he shall forever be,

A great man to see.

The legend I’m of such a fan,

Is the one and only Jackie Chan.
